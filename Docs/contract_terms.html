SECURITY

Metro Fun Center reserves the right to inspect and control all events, and may, at the discretion of Management, require security for certain events, particularly those with underage guests in attendance. The cost of such services will be the client's responsibility. Metro Fun Center will not assume any liability for theft or damage to personal property occurring prior to, during, or after the event. Underage guests (under 21) are not permitted after 9:00pm.
